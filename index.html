ISABELA STATE UNIVERSITY
CITY OF ILAGAN CAMPUS

Isabela State University-Ilagan Campus was one of the three (3) original campuses merged to become the first state university in Region II, the others were Cabagan Campus and Echague Campus. Through the Presidential Decree 1434 signed on June 10,1978, the Isabela State University was created. Today there are eleven campuses of the university system strategically located in the different municipalities of Isabela.

6 ISU-I prof join new Region 2 roster of Accredited Full-Fledge Professors

Led by Zonal President and RASUC chair Dr. Ricmar P. Aquino and Zonal Director for Region 02 Dr. Boyet L, Batang, thirty-nine (39) newly Accredited Full-Fledged professors from the Isabela State University (ISU) System took part in the commemorative signing, oath taking, hooding, and medal vesting at the Isabela Convention Center (ICON), Cauayan City, Isabela, November 13. Among the thirty-nine (39) ISU professors out of fifty-two (52) professors from the region who passed the screening of the Philippine Association of State Universities and Colleges (PASUC) Zonal Faculty Evaluation and Computerization Center - Region 02 and the Regional Accreditation Committee, six (6) are from the ISU - City of Ilagan Campus. Prof. Michelle G. Quijano, PhD, Prof. Helen T. Asio, PhD, Prof. Ma. Cristina Lalaine M. Nerona, PhD, Prof. Cesar Vallejo, PhD, Prof. Drolly P. Claravall, EdD, and Prof. Carina Vidania, PhD were conferred by the said accrediting body as Full-Fledged Professors. Full Professor is the highest rank that a professor can achieve with the goal of expanding their research, building their body of work even further, and often taking a leadership role in the department for departmental and extra-departmental administrative tasks.

ISU-I wins 1st spot in provincial DTI comp

Along with the celebration of the Consumer Welfare Month 2022 last October, the Department of Trade and Industry (DTI) - Isabela conducted the 2022 Isabela Consumer Cup (ICC) which opened contested activities like virtual quiz show for both public and private Junior High School and Senior High School students in Isabela and infomercial-making competition for all Isabeleño youth aging 18-30 years old. The Newsette Publication (TN) Editor-in-Chief John David Calagui, TN Head TV Broadcaster Kenneth Agub, and University Sports Development Office Staff Engr. Mark Christian Fernandez represented the Isabela State University - City of Ilagan Campus on the Infomercial-making competition against eleven (11) other teams from the province. The team won first (1st) place and received twelve thousand pesos (P12,000) along with a certificate and plaque of recognition, Best Technical Application award, and an SM advantage card at the Technical Education and Skills Development Authority - Isabela School of Arts and Trades (TESDA-ISAT), City of Ilagan, Isabela, this afternoon. DTI is set to open the ICC 2023 next year to offer more contested activities for the youth to promote consumerism in the province.
